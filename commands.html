General Commands

Ping: Shows your ping to the server.

Say: Repeats what you said.

Discrim: Searches for a discriminator (the number after the # in your name)

Tag: Lets you create and view other users tags.

Google: Shows the top result on Google of what you searched for.

Weather: Shows you the current weather of the location you specified.

Support: Links you to Maestoso's support server.

Invite: Invites Maestoso to your server.

Upvote: Links you to Discord Bot List where you can upvote Maestoso.

Stats: Shows Maestoso's stats, ie uptime and server count.

Serverinfo: Shows server information such as member count.

Userinfo: Shows the information of a user.

Ascii: Writes your text with slashes.

Avatar: Shows the avatar picture of a user.

Music Commands

Play: Plays a link or a song name.

Stop: Stops the current song that's playing.

Skip: Skips the current song that's playing.

Join: Join the voice channel that you're in.

Leave: Makes Maestoso leave the channel that it's in.

Pause: Pauses the song that is currently playing.

Resume: Resumes the paused song.

Queue: Shows all the songs that are in the queue.

Currently: Shows the song that is currently playing.

Volume: Allows you to change the volume of the song that's currently playing.

Fun Commands

Clever: Lets you talk to a clever bot.

Knock: Plays a high quality knocking sound (good for trolling)

Flipcoin: Flips a coin for you.

8ball: Gives an answer to your yes or no question.

Cowsay: Try it for yourself.

Dice: Rolls a number from 1 to 6.

Calmdown: Gives you a little animation of emojis calming down.

Joke: Gives you a good joke or just a really bad pun.

Currency Commands

Daily: Gives you a daily bonus of 100 credits.

Credits: Shows you the current amoung of credits you have.

Mod Commands

Poll: Creates a yes or no poll.

Prefix: Lets you set a custom prefix on your server.

Kick: Kicks the mentioned user.

Ban: Bans the mentioned user.

Purge: Bulk deletes messages.

Config: Allows you to see the server config.

Toggleinvites: Allows you to turn on invite prevention.
